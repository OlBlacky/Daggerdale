## Shadow Melancholia

Almost all non-Shadowfell natives will suffer from Shadow Melancholia
within an hour of arrival in the plain. This manifests as an inescapable
sense of cold and despair. It effects creatures from different planes
and different natures to varying degrees;

### Prime Material Inhabitants

- -1 to all saving throws, -2 to their worst saving throw

- -4 to save vs. fear and negative emotion effects (total, does not
  stack with the above)

- -1 to all skill rolls

- -2 to all Dex and Cha skill rolls

### Fey Inhabitants (including Jasper and Jeska)

- -2 to all saving throws, -3 to their worst saving throw

- -4 to save vs. fear and negative emotion effects (total, does not
  stack with the above)

- -2 to all skill rolls

- -3 to all Dex and Cha skill rolls

## ![](/Daggerdale/images/chapter7/image81.jpeg)Light and Heat

- Mundane sources of light emit dim light to either their bright light
  radius or ½ their dim light radius, whichever is greater.

- Mundane sources of fire/heat damage burn half as hot, doing ½ damage

- Divine light sources work as follows

  - Less than 5<sup>th</sup> level does not function

  - 5<sup>th</sup> level function like mundane light sources

- Divine sources of fire/heat work as follows

  - Less than 5<sup>th</sup> level does not function

  - 5<sup>th</sup> level function like mundane fire/heat sources

- Arcane light sources work as follows

  - 1-4<sup>th</sup> level function like mundane sources

  - 5<sup>th</sup> level function normally

- Arcane fire/heat works as follows

  - 1-4<sup>th</sup> level function like mundane sources

  - 5<sup>th</sup> level function normally

- Mundane healing works at ½ effect

- Divine sources of healing work as follows

  - 1-4<sup>th</sup> level heal with ½ effect

  - 5<sup>th</sup> level heals with -1 per die or level

- Arcane sources of healing work as follows

  - 1-4<sup>th</sup> level heal with -1 per die or level

  - 5<sup>th</sup> level works normally
