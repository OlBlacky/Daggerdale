## The Circle of the Dead

*You've be following an old path that winds its way through the forest
atop the escarpment. It occasionally goes right to the edge of the
cliff, giving you a clear look over the Tesh valley, and the Dagger
Hills beyond. The morning is fair and cool. There is a bit of frost on
the ground, which gives your boots a satisfying squishy crunch with
every step. The air is fragrant, and songbirds are everywhere. It is
hard to imagine a finer spring morning.*

*However, at one point the trail turns north for a considerable run,
avoiding some rough terrain, and dives through a thick copse of
evergreen trees. The smell of the firs and balsams is pleasing, and Mari
has been delighted to find so many mushrooms of great quality growing in
the shade of these thick trees. Emerging from the other side of the
copse, the pleasant morning gives way to an earie scene. A torn up camp
is before you, and there is a very unnatural looking circle around a
limestone outcropping that appears to be dead and twisted. From the
other side of the thick trunk of large and twisted tree in the middle,
you catch a flicker of movement. A bare human arm is moving listlessly,
and you can see that there is a person tied to the tree, though they are
on the other side of it from you.*
