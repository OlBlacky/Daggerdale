By Carrier Dove.

Captain Monarch;

2 platoons of the 18<sup>th</sup> Company are currently making for Greenwood Farm
and should arrive well before the sun goes down. They are being led by
Captain Renley Rawthorn himself. 1<sup>st</sup> platoon will stay a tenday, at
our cost.

2<sup>nd</sup> platoon is lead by Lt. Williams Waterborne and will stay with you
three tenday at your cost, per the Provisions of the Common Defense. I
am authorizing 2<sup>nd</sup> platoon to stay as long as a year, if you desire,
and will consider re-commissioning this platoon as the 7<sup>th</sup> Company
5<sup>th</sup> Platoon Bright Spears, if both you and 7<sup>th</sup> the Company Captain
desire it, during Muster next year.

Captain Garrick Greenfields of the 10<sup>th</sup> Company will reinforce the
southern flank of the Tower with a full platoon, and has been given
tactical command to decide how to deploy his men in your battle against
the Torn Cheek. This Ride will be supplied at our cost, per the
Provisions.

Cpt. Greenfields also has a train with the requested alchemical
supplies, supplied by our armoury in Towne, which we donate to the
cause.

May the sun find your blades held high on the morrow,

Gen. D.Thunderstorm.
