From the Yellow Abbeye Booke of Surveys and Measures, 1481 Edition

## Whitegate Pedigree

- Ardelusk (1340-1399) -- Marlee Dale (1360-1377)

  - Loedegan (1375-1455) -- Sarah Weaver (1383-1403)

    - Luminous **(Lord of Castle Radiance and High Commander of the
      Order of the Lambent Shield)** (1399-) -- Raynalla Proudholme
      (1410-1429)

      - Solis (1429-1455)

    - Ardelusk (1400-1455) -- Rayannya Proudholme (1399-)

      - Blaze (1416-) **(Bishop of Daggerfalls)--** Dorabell Archer
        (1426-1455)

        - Lucidus (1444-1480) -- Sarah Forster (1460-)

  - Loedegan -- Becca Weaver (2^nd^ wife; 1381-1417)

    - Brilliance (F, 1404-1470) Never married, no children

    - Radiance (F, 1409-1439) -- Karrigan Thunderstorm (1400-1476)

      - Dauntless Thunderstorm (**General of the Sun Blades**) (1426-)

      - This is a prolific side of the family, with lots of
        Thunderstorms issuing from the union of Radiance and Karrigan.

  - Loedegan -- Taryara Millstone (3^rd^ wife; 1400-1455)

    - Argent **(Zenith Lord of Daggerdale)** (1419-) -- Sarah Mourn
      (1420-1475)

      - Perspicuus (1440-1477) -- Lanya Forster (1440-1480)

        - Lumina (1455-)

        - Lumen (1457-1480)

        - Luminosus (1458-1480)

      - Aestus (F. 1441-1478) -- Decker Blackmane (1429-)

        - The Blackmane side of the family is also prolific.

  - Madigan (1377-1455) (Madigan had only one illegitimate daughters,
    and now no surviving members)
