Received by messenger at the Myth Drannor Embassy, late morning Ches 21,
1483 DR.

My Dearest Jeska,

I trust this letter finds you in the bloom of good health and spirits.
It is with great delight and anticipation that I extend to you a cordial
invitation to a gathering of mirth and splendor, to be held within the
comfort of my humble abode three nights hence.

In contemplating this forthcoming celebration, my thoughts have often
turned to you, dear Jeska, as a cherished beacon of grace and charm
within our esteemed town. Your presence, like a gentle zephyr amid a
verdant garden, is regarded as a treasure among our circle of
acquaintances.

It is my earnest hope that you shall honour me by gracing the soirée
with your esteemed presence. Pray, bring with you any friends whose
company you hold dear, that they too may partake in the myriad delights
that shall be laid before us.

The evening promises to be one of enchantment and camaraderie, where
laughter shall mingle with the strains of melodious music, and where the
flickering glow of candlelight shall cast its soft enchantment upon all
in attendance.

Anticipating the pleasure of your company, dear Jeska, I remain, with
warm regards,

Yours affectionately,

LC.
